Who am I?

Hello! I'm Tomás Leguizamón, a technology and programming enthusiast. Since my studies at Colegio Corazonista and Lausana, I have explored the world of software development, combining creativity and logic to bring innovative projects to life.

I specialize in creating functional applications, with experience in systems such as Firebase, interface development, and hardware control. One of my standout projects is a pet food dispenser, where I integrated electronics and software to improve pet care.

Additionally, I enjoy flight simulation in Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020, where I recreate real flights and develop airport modifications. I’m also passionate about gaming, optimizing performance in titles like GTA V, CS2, and Fortnite.

I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to continue learning and growing in the world of technology.
